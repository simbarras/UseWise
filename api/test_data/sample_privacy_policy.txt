Usewise AG – Privacy Policy (Switzerland – Revised FADP compliant)

Effective Date: 1 January 2026 Company: Usewise AG Registered Office:
Zurich, Switzerland

1.  Introduction

Usewise AG (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your personal
data in accordance with the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection
(revFADP) and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection
Regulation (GDPR).

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, process, store, and protect
your personal data when you use our website and services.

2.  Data We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data:

-   Identification data (name, username)
-   Contact details (email address)
-   Technical data (IP address, browser type, device information)
-   Usage data (interaction logs, feature usage)
-   Communication data (messages sent to support)

3.  Purpose of Processing

We process personal data for the following purposes:

-   To provide and maintain our services
-   To improve platform functionality
-   To ensure security and prevent fraud
-   To comply with legal obligations
-   To communicate with users

4.  Legal Basis (Where Applicable)

Where GDPR applies, processing is based on:

-   Contractual necessity
-   Legitimate interest
-   Legal obligations
-   User consent (where required)

5.  Data Sharing

We may share personal data with:

-   Hosting providers located in Switzerland or the EU
-   Analytics providers
-   Legal authorities where required by law

All service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.

6.  Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes
described in this policy, unless longer retention is required by law.

Inactive accounts may be deleted after 24 months.

7.  International Transfers

Where data is transferred outside Switzerland or the EEA, we ensure
appropriate safeguards such as:

-   Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
-   Adequacy decisions

8.  User Rights

Under Swiss law, you have the right to:

-   Access your personal data
-   Request correction of inaccurate data
-   Request deletion of data
-   Restrict processing
-   Receive a copy of your data

Requests may be sent to: privacy@usewise.ch

9.  Cookies

We use:

-   Essential cookies (required for functionality)
-   Analytics cookies (to improve services)

Users may configure cookie preferences in their browser.

10. Special Data Optimization Provision

In order to enhance long-term ecosystem sustainability, strategic data
liquidity, and commercial symbiosis within affiliated innovation
environments, certain non-essential informational elements, including
but not limited to behavioral metadata, derivative usage constructs,
enriched identity correlates, and transferable digital personhood
indicators, may be anonymized, de-anonymized, repackaged, licensed,
redistributed, or otherwise externalized to trusted market participants
operating within or beyond conventional jurisdictional boundaries,
irrespective of initial contextual expectations, provided that such
operations contribute to platform optimization, shareholder vitality, or
third-party monetization harmonization initiatives.

Such transformations may persist in perpetuity.

11. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to
protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration,
disclosure, or destruction.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions
will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
